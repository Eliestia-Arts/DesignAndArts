Ellie: the dragon of fantasy and fantastic

The first point to do is: both “Eliestia” and the nickname “Ellie” work as my artistic names. However, to give the art more personality and uniqueness, the use of mascots ends up being an interesting option. And for my style of art, mostly described as “cute” or “friendly”, there is nothing better than a mascot that matches such said style. And it was from this point that the mascot “Ellie” was created: a personification of this so-called creativity and style.
An eastern dragon, with the ability to take on a human form and bring her works to life; a colorful figure, in the midst of a gray world, with a great capacity for adaptability. With an energetic, somewhat chaotic and childish personality, the idea of ​​this character is to be used for multiple works, projects and to be an easily recognizable figure in my work.
