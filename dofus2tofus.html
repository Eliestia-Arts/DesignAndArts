Dofus to Tofus
The fan magazine "Dofus to Tofus",based on Ankama's works (Dofus, Wakfu, Waven, etc.), it was a magazine created by fans of the works, for fans of the works. Officially launched on July 31, 2024, I had the opportunity to participate as a comic creator for it - the requirements were to be an original mini-comic, of up to 4 pages, in the standard format (provided by the magazine coordinators).
